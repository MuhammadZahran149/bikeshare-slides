How are the daily trips distributed according to time of the day?¶

Trips are distributed through the day as a bimodal distribution with two extreme peaks at 8am and 17 pm, That distribution is constant through week days that may be due to people using the service to go to and back from work
With Weekends distribution is normally distributed with no peaks at a certain hour

Is every user a subscriber?¶

Conclusions¶

  • Most users of the service are male users
  • Most users are subscribers
  • The age group that uses the service the most are of ages 18-35 and the complete absence of people under 18
  • Users use the service the most at hours 9 am and 7pm
  • Users use the service the most on Wednesday
  • Users normally doesn't take bike share for all trip
  • The most frequent starting and ending stations are based on the west, as you go more to the east the tendency for people to use the service becomes less

Recommendations:¶

  • Company should target to increase the users from the females of all ages
  • Teenagers of below 18 years old should be targeted and explore the cause of their complete absence in the data set
  • Weekends should be targeted more to increase the usage of service
  • Eastern and central parts of the city should be targeted to increase the usage of the service
